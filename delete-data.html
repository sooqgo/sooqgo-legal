How to Delete Your SOOQGO Account & Data

App Name: SOOQGO — Auto Parts Marketplace UAE
Developer: SOOQGO
Last Updated: May 13, 2026

SOOQGO respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data.

Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)

Send an email to our support team with the subject "Delete My Account":

📧 sooqgo.ae@gmail.com
Step 1 — Compose the Email Use the subject line: Delete My Account
Step 2 — Include These Details In the email body, provide:
  • The email address associated with your SOOQGO account
  • Your display name on the app (if you remember it)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
Step 3 — Send and Wait We will confirm your request within 48 hours and complete the deletion within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

Method 2: In-App (Coming Soon)

In a future update, you will be able to delete your account directly from the app via Settings → Account → Delete Account.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon receiving your verified request, we will delete:

Data TypeAction
Account information (name, email, phone)Permanently deleted
Profile photoPermanently deleted
Your shop information and listingsPermanently deleted
Photos you uploaded for listingsPermanently deleted
Chat messages you sentDeleted from your account; recipients keep their chat history copy
Part requests you sentPermanently deleted
Favorites and saved itemsPermanently deleted
FCM push notification tokenPermanently deleted

What Data Is Retained (and Why)

The following data may be retained for limited periods, as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

Data TypeRetention PeriodReason
Anonymized analyticsUp to 14 monthsAggregate usage statistics (no personal identifiers)
Backup copies on secure serversUp to 30 days after deletionDisaster recovery purposes only
Transaction records (if any)Up to 5 yearsUAE tax and legal compliance
Records of moderation actions (bans, reports)IndefinitePrevent abuse and protect community safety

After the retention period expires, residual data is automatically and permanently purged from our systems.

Partial Data Deletion

If you want to delete only specific data (not the entire account) — for example, removing a listing, a chat conversation, or your profile photo — you can do this directly within the app:

  • Listings: Open My Shop → Tap the listing → Delete
  • Chat: Long-press the chat → Delete conversation
  • Profile photo: Profile → Edit → Remove photo
  • Anything else: Email us at sooqgo.ae@gmail.com

Contact

Questions about data deletion? Reach us at sooqgo.ae@gmail.com

الطريقة 1: طلب عبر البريد الإلكتروني (موصى به)

أرسل إيميل لفريق الدعم بعنوان "Delete My Account":

الخطوة 3 — أرسل وانتظر سنؤكد طلبك خلال 48 ساعة، ونكمل الحذف خلال 7 أيام عمل. ستصلك رسالة تأكيد لما يتم الحذف.

في تحديث قادم، ستقدر تحذف حسابك مباشرة من التطبيق عبر: الإعدادات ← الحساب ← حذف الحساب.
